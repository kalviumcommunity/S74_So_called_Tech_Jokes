** So-Called Technical Jokes ** 


## Project Overview  
**So-Called Technical Jokes** is a light-hearted platform designed to bring a smile to the faces of developers by showcasing unique, tech-themed humor. Whether you're a Front-End wizard, Back-End architect, Full-Stack hero, or an AI/ML enthusiast, there's something here to make you laugh.  

The platform offers a curated collection of jokes tailored to different developer roles and allows users to rate, share, and contribute their own humor. It's a fun and interactive way to build a sense of community among developers while providing some much-needed comic relief.  

---

## Key Features  

### **Joke Categorization by Developer Type**  
- Jokes tailored to specific developer roles, including:  
  - **Front-End Developers**  
  - **Back-End Developers**  
  - **Full-Stack Developers**  
  - **AI/ML Developers**  
  - And more!  
- Each category addresses relatable and humorous scenarios unique to these roles.  

### **Interactive Feedback System**  
- Users can vote on jokes as **"Funny"** or **"Not Funny"**.  
- A live leaderboard highlights the top-rated jokes across all categories.  

### **User-Generated Content**  
- Developers can submit their own jokes to keep the content fresh and engaging.  
- All submissions are moderated before being displayed.  

### **Responsive Design**  
- Fully optimized for both desktop and mobile devices to ensure accessibility for users everywhere.  

### **Social Sharing Options**  
- Users can share their favorite jokes on social media platforms, spreading the fun to others.
